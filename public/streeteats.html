Street Eats: the food truck locator (Link to GitHub repo)
Street Eats is a mobile app for food truck lovers in San Diego built for a local entrepreneur. The app allows food truck owners to register their food trucks, create a profile with menu and broadcast their current location at all times. Customers can filter food trucks, locate them in real time and rate their experience on the app. The app was built with JavaScript, React Native, Expo, MongoDB, Loopback, Socket.io and RESTful APIs. You can view the GitHub repo here or download it from the App Store soon!
